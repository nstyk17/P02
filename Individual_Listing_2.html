Description

Introducing our ferociously adorable T-Rex costume for cats! Transform your furry friend into the king of the prehistoric jungle with this eye-catching ensemble.Featuring intricate details such as realistic T-Rex arms, menacing teeth, and a spiky tail, this costume is guaranteed to turn heads at any occasion. The adjustable straps and our custom fitting ensures a snug and secure fit for cats of all sizes, from petite felines to larger breeds.

Materials & Care

Materials: Our T-Rex costume for cats is crafted from soft and durable polyester fabric, featuring high-quality stitching and adjustable straps for comfort and longevity, while intricate details such as T-Rex arms, teeth, and tail are designed for authenticity. Care Instructions: To maintain the costume's quality, hand wash gently with mild detergent and cold water, air dry completely before storing, avoid ironing or bleach, store in a cool, dry place away from sunlight, and regularly inspect for wear or tear, repairing as needed for your cat's safety and comfort.
