Our Story:

With a combined passion for costuming, dinsosaurs, and pets; at Pawsaurus Clothing Co we are determined to match you and your best furred friend with the purrrfect costume for any occasion!

We are a group of theater costume designers who found a shared passion to craft costumes creatures and critters of all shapes and sizes and decided to found a company that allows our passions to flourish.
